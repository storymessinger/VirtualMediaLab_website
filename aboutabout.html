Working with Visual Media Lab

The Visual Media Lab (VML) in the Graduate School of Culture Technology (GSCT) at KAIST is where technology and creativity meet. VML focuses on developing fundamental computer graphics technology and software applications specifically for the creation of movie visual effects and animated films.
