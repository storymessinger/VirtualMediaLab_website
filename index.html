Visual Media Lab.

Where Creativity Meets Technology


We focuses on developing fundamental computer graphics technology and software applications specifically for the creation of movie visual effects and animated films. Since its foundation in 2006, VML has emphasized both R&D and contens production as a core part of lab activity. About 20 Ph.D. and master program students along have published numerous papers at premium graphics conferences and showcased animations at international computer animated festivals. The close relationship with industry has allowed VML to contribute its technology to recently released movies.
